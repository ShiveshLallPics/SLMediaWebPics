About Me

Hey there! I’m Shivesh,welcome to my website!

My journey with photography began in 2019, and over the past four years, I’ve had the incredible opportunity to work professionally in the industry, specializing in wedding photography. Along the way, I’ve explored many genres, from the intimacy of weddings to the thrill of automotive photography.

But for me, photography is so much more than just taking pictures,it’s about freezing a moment in time, capturing emotions, and telling stories that words sometimes can’t. Nothing compares to the joy of seeing someone’s face light up when they relive a special moment through my work.

I’d love to help tell your story through my lens. Feel free to reach out,I look forward to connecting with you!
